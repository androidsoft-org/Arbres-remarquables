The paulownia (Paulownia tomentosa Steudel) planted in 1895, is remarkable for its great age.

Family of Paulowniaceae, it is native to northern China.

In 2011, this tree was 20 meters high with a trunk circumference of 4.20 meters.

Native to Central and South China

other name: Tomentose because its branches are tomentose (silky).

"Paulownia" in reference to the origins of the Russian princess Anna Pavlovna (1795-1865), daughter of Tsar Paul and the first passion for gardens.

The first paulownia planted in Europe, was at the Jardin des Plantes in Paris in 1834

Do not miss the blue flowering very fragrant in early May.
