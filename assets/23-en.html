This despair monkeys (Araucaria araucana, Koch), planted in 1907, is remarkable for its age.

It belongs to the family Araucariaceae, native to Chile.

In 2011, this tree was 9 meters high with a trunk circumference of 1.40 meters.

Native conifer native to the Andes. Introduced in France by Neumann in 1837 in the Jardin des Plantes in Paris

This is the national tree of Chile.

Its sharp leaves, rigid and sharp explain his name despair monkeys. Indeed the monkeys can not access its fruits.

The seeds of Araucaria araucana are edible and are widely used in Chile.
