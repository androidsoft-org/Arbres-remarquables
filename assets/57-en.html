This common plane (Platanus x acerifolia botanist, Willd.), Planted in 1860, is remarkable for its height and age.

Hybrid between the Oriental plane tree and the plane of the West, it belongs to the family of Platanaceae.

In 2011, this tree was 45 meters high with a trunk circumference of 4.10 meters.

Also called hybrid Spain Plane (Platanus x hispanica) where they were introduced and hybridized. Then called to Sycamore maple leaves distinguished in France in 1703 by Tournefort.

Hybrid of Western sycamore (Platanus occidentalis) and Oriental plane tree (Platanus orientalis).

Tree widely used in cities where we do not hesitate to impose severe pruning.
