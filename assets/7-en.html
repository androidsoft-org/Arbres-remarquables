This Oriental plane tree (Platanus orientalis, L.), planted in 1814, is remarkable for his age and six trunks clump.

It belongs to the family of Platanaceae, from south-eastern Europe. In 2011, this tree was 20 meters high with a trunk circumference of 7 meters.

Native to East Asia. In France the species is listed as from 1648. This is one of the oldest tree species grown. The species most elegant (cutting the foliage).

This is the essence in which meetings were held of the philosophers of ancient Greece. Its origin (genetic) explains its resistance to hot and dry: it is therefore a candidate for global warming

Symbol of regeneration and fertility, the plane represents the mother goddess because of its palmate leaf like a hand into a ball of its fruit full of seed as an ovary.
