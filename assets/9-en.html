This ailanthe (Ailanthus giraldii, Dode) is remarkable for its impressive harbor.

Of the family Simaroubaceae, is native to China.

In 2011, this tree was 35 meters high with a trunk circumference of 4.60 meters.

A native of western China. Discovered in 1895 by Father G. Giraldi Italian missionary who reaped many new palntes in Shensi.

The family name (Simaroubaceae) has Simaruba amara, the Caribbean white wood, bark bitter, tonic and febrifuge.
